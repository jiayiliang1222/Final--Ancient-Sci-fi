The alien finally comes.
The soldiers surround the Emperor with its coils wrapped around steel posts and the rakes of spears used to poke holes in the walls. They tear open the gates of the fortress like giant drapes, and the Emperor is taken back into the darkness of space.

Around them, the stars glowed with a silvery glow similar to the cyan moon in the morning, and soon the entire sky was covered by the glowing crescendos.
